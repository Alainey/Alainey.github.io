About Me



Hi there! My name is Alainey Hellman. I'm a fourth year concentrator at Brown University studying Computer Science. As a front end developer, I'm particularly interested in the integration of technology and art, and I aim to produce interactive work that showcases an intersection of the two.

This site was originally created as a simple digital portfolio for the course CS 1300 - User Interfaces and User Experiences. The site features pieces from this class, alongside personal projects and sculpture work. I am now an undergraduate TA for this course, helping to both update the course curriculum and provide instruction for this year's students.

Feel free to reach out to me here, or elsewhere on social media.
